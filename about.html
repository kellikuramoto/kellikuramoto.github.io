Who am I?

Junior @ Carnegie Mellon University

B.S. Information Systems and Additional Major in Human Compter Interaction

I believe the combination of my technical and non traditional creative background makes me a stronger designer. I strive to embrace a variety of perspectives when addressing problems with a human centered solution.

How did I get here?

Growing up, I never knew what I wanted to do but I knew I wanted to help people.

In highschool I started to drift torwards political science. My first internship was with the local congressman, working to pass a proposal on increased subsized housing for low income families.

However, once I got to college I fell in love with human centered design process and ux design. In my classes, I realized that there were so many ways that I could help people.

Where am I going?

I thrive in a collaborative environment where multiple perspectives are take when tacking any problem.

I am always seeking to help others through my design and have make efforts torwards more accessible design.
